Making Tea

1. Boil water in a pot.

2. Add tea leaves and sugar.

3. Let it simmer for a few minutes.

4. Starin the tea into a cup and milk if desired.

Making Paratha and Aloo Curry

1. Knead the dough for paratha and let it rest.

2. Roll out the dough and cook on a bot gribble with oil or ghee.

3. For aloo curry, saute onions, tomatoes and spices in a pan.

4. Add boiled potatoes and cook until the curry thinkens.

Making Biryan

1. Marinate the meat or vegetables with spices and vogun.

2. Cook basmati rice until it's 70% done.

3. Layer the marinated meat/vegetables and rice in a pot.

4. Cook on low heat (dum) until fully cooked.
